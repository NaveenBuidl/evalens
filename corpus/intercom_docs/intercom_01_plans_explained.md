Title: Intercom plans explained

URL Source: https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061614-intercom-plans-explained

Markdown Content:
Intercom’s plans are designed to give you clarity and flexibility as your support needs grow. You can choose from the Essential, Advanced, or Expert plan, and move between them at any time. Each teammate requires a seat, and the plan you select determines the seat price. Advanced and Expert also include lite seats for teammates who need limited access.

## Seat pricing

The seat price depends on the plan you choose. You can purchase as many seats as you need (there are no minimums or maximums).

## Usage-based charges

Fin AI Agent costs $0.99 per [outcome](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205718-fin-ai-agent-resolutions) on all plans. This pricing applies to Fin over chat and email. To use Fin over phone (Fin Voice) and for pricing details, please contact Sales.

Pay-as-you-go charges also apply for:
- Email campaigns

- SMS

- WhatsApp

- Phone

Every plan has access to these channels, and you only pay for what you use.

## Add-ons

## Pro

The [Pro add-on](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/13868265-pro-add-on) enables you to see and continuously improve 100% of your customer conversations, so you can scale Fin with confidence. Get a clear picture of 100% of your customer conversations. Check if quality meets your standards and get prioritized fixes to keep improving the experience your team delivers.

## Copilot unlimited usage

[Copilot](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9121374-fin-ai-copilot-explained) is a personal AI assistant designed to revolutionize support agent efficiency. All full-seat teammates have access to Copilot with usage included up to 10 conversations per month. You can purchase [unlimited usage](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9121384-fin-ai-copilot-included-and-unlimited-usage#h_270b1c71d3) as a flexible per-seat add-on on any plan.

## Proactive Support Plus

[Proactive Support Plus](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus) is designed to streamline your outbound efforts with extra features specifically designed for Proactive Support. You’ll be charged a flat fee per month plus usage-based charges for [Messages Sent](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus#h_45c5377e15), if you exceed your monthly allowance. You can purchase this add-on for any plan.

## Services

With these optional service add-ons, you can gain access to personalized onboarding and priority customer support.
- Premier Onboarding - Gives access to an onboarding specialist to provide custom onboarding help for a one-time fee.

- Premier Support - Gives access to faster response times, priority escalations, video chat, and greater availability from our CS team for a monthly recurring fee.

Contact Sales if you’re interested in one of these services.

## Fin AI Agent for your existing helpdesk

If you use Zendesk, Salesforce, or another external helpdesk, you can purchase **Fin AI Agent** without an Intercom plan.

Pricing:
- $0.99 per outcome

- Minimum commitments apply

- No seat costs or hidden platform fees

Features include:
- Quick setup with your current helpdesk

- Answers email, live chat, phone, and more

- Customizable tone and answer length

- Ability to take actions on external systems

- Seamless handoff to agents in your helpdesk

[Start a free trial](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) - no credit card required. During the 14-day trial, you’ll have access to your chosen plan and Fin AI Agent, along with the Pro, Proactive Support Plus and Copilot add-ons.

After the free trial period, simply confirm the plan you want, and add your payment details to continue using Intercom.

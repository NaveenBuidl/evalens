## Pricing overview

### How does Intercom pricing work?

Pricing depends on whether you’re purchasing our full Customer Service Suite, or purchasing Fin AI Agent to use with your existing helpdesk (such as Zendesk, Salesforce, and others).

**Pricing for Intercom's Customer Service Suite:**

When you choose Intercom’s Customer Service Suite, you can select from three plans: Essential, Advanced, or Expert. The plan you choose determines your seat price. You’ll then be charged based on the number of [full seats](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205716-seats) you need—these provide full access to all plan features and are billed per teammate.

All plans include access to:

*   Intercom Helpdesk

*   Fin AI Agent

**Pricing for Fin on your existing helpdesk:**

If you’re using Fin with a helpdesk like Zendesk or Salesforce, we offer separate pricing:

*   $0.99 per Fin outcome

*   Minimum commitments apply

*   Unlimited teammates – No seat costs

There are no extra charges for integration, setup, or platform use when using Fin with your existing helpdesk.

### What is Proactive Support Plus?

[Proactive Support Plus](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on) is an optional add-on available for any Intercom plan (Essential, Advanced, or Expert).

It unlocks advanced capabilities like Product Tours, Surveys, In-app Posts, Mobile Push, and Mobile Carousels. The add-on is priced at $99 per month, which includes 500 messages sent each month. If you exceed 500 messages in a month, additional charges will apply based on the ["Messages Sent"](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on#h_45c5377e15) metric.

You can add Proactive Support Plus to your plan at any time unless you are on a contract, in which case you’ll need to contact your Account Manager.

### Are there any additional usage charges?

In addition to seats, you only pay for what you use. Here’s a breakdown:

**WhatsApp:**

WhatsApp is charged per ‘conversation’, with unit costs varying by volume. We have different pricing for inbound and outbound WhatsApp. [Learn more about WhatsApp pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels#h_13e2dcaf16).

**SMS:**

SMS is charged per the number of “SMS sent and/or received” with unit costs varying by volume. Prices also vary by region. [Learn more about SMS pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels#h_ee3403f06b).

**Email Campaigns:**

Emails sent from the Inbox have no additional charge. Inbound emails from your customers and responses to those emails are included in your plan, with no additional charge. Outbound email campaigns are charged per the number of ‘Bulk Emails Sent’. [Learn more about Email pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels#h_6fdc658267).

**Fin Outcomes:**

Fin AI Agent usage is measured in 'Outcomes.' This ensures that you only pay when Fin delivers a successful result. An Outcome is counted when the customer confirms the AI Answer resolved their issue, or the customer doesn’t request more help after the last AI Answer or Fin successfully executes a Procedure configured to end in a handoff to a human or workflow (a Procedure Handoff). [Learn more about Fin AI Agent pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205718-fin-resolutions-and-custom-answers).

**Proactive Support Plus & Messages Sent:**

Purchasing the optional ‘Proactive Support Plus’ add-on unlocks a number of capabilities including Product Tours, Surveys, In-app Posts, Mobile Push, and Mobile Carousels. The baseline monthly fee includes 500 Messages Sent every month (see below for how ‘Messages Sent’ is calculated). Any messages sent above 500 per month are at an additional cost and charged per ‘Message Sent.’ [Learn more about Proactive Support Plus](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus).

Use our intuitive [Pricing Calculator](https://www.intercom.com/pricing/calculator) to effortlessly project your expenses and gain clarity on how Intercom fits within your budget.

### What apps and integrations does Intercom offer?

### Do I have to sign up with a contract?

No, all plans offer a yearly subscription and you can also choose to pay monthly on the Essential and Advanced plans. If you'd prefer to have a contract, this can be arranged with our Sales team.

### Can I create my own customized package?

You can choose your core plan, your number of seats, and if you want any add-ons. You can also choose whether to use Fin, Email, WhatsApp, Phone, and SMS. You cannot add something from one plan to another nor remove something from a plan.

### What’s the minimum I have to buy to use Intercom?

All our plans require the purchase of only a single seat to get started. Unless you have a contracted subscription, all usage is pay-as-you-go and you only pay for what you use. So it's easy to get started and grow with us over time.

## Seats

### What is a seat

[Seats](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205716-intercom-seats-full-seats-and-lite-seats) provide access to Intercom’s products and features within your chosen plan. Each teammate using Intercom must have a designated seat assigned to them.

*   [Lite seats](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205716-intercom-seats#h_b37e53dd64) provide limited access to Intercom to support cross-functional collaboration. A set number of Lite seats are included without additional charge only on the Advanced and Expert plans.

### How can I change my plan or number of seats?

Customers on pay monthly/yearly subscriptions can easily [manage their plan, seats, and add-ons](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8344189-how-to-upgrade-or-downgrade-from-a-plan) from their Intercom workspace. Customers on contracts need to contact their Account Manager to make a change.

To get access to Premier Support, Premier Onboarding, and Custom API rate limits, you'll need to talk to Sales and sign a contract.

### Does a lite seat include access to Proactive Support Plus?

A lite seat grants access to Proactive Support Plus settings, but not the ability to create content or set it live (outbound messages and Series). A full seat is needed in order to create and manage outbound content.

### Does a lite seat include access to Copilot?

No, a lite seat can't use Copilot (included or unlimited usage).

### Are there any additional usage charges?

In addition to seats and add-ons, you only pay for what you use. See a pricing breakdown for [usage-based channels](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels) and [Fin AI Agent Outcomes](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205718-fin-resolutions). Use our intuitive [Pricing Calculator](https://www.intercom.com/pricing/calculator) to effortlessly project your expenses and gain clarity on how Intercom fits within your budget. Proactive Support Plus costs $99/month and includes 500 messages; additional usage-based charges apply for usage beyond 500 messages. It offers features such as Series, Checklists, News, A/B Testing, Webhooks, and Versioning. Usage-based features include Posts, Mobile Push, Product Tours, Surveys, and Mobile Carousels. Pricing is prorated when the add-on is activated mid-month. Usage-based fees typically follow tiered rates, meaning higher usage results in a lower cost per unit. This makes it more economical for extensive usage levels.

## Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards for online payment as well as Apple Pay / Google Pay and [Stripe Link](https://link.com/ie). Invoices cannot be paid via debit cards, pre-paid card, PayPal or similar services.

Customers on a contract are given the option of ACH transfers and credit card payments. If you have a contracted subscription, your contract will show the agreed payment method e.g. credit card, ACH or wire transfer.

### Do you accept ACH / Wire transfer payments?

ACH / Wire transfer payments are available only to customers who are on a contracted subscription. Your contract will state the agreed payment method.

Our banking for ACH / Wire transfers are JPMorgan Chase Bank and Wells Fargo.

If you are sending payments for multiple workspaces, please send them as separate ACH transactions (do not combine them into a single payment).

### Can you refund my payment and charge a different card instead?

We cannot refund a payment and then charge a different card. If you’ve updated your payment method in your [Billing settings](https://app.intercom.com/a/apps/_/billing/settings), the new card will be used for all future payments.

### Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes, startups get 90% off Intercom + 1 year of Fin free. Find out more about Intercom's [Early Stage](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9361928-early-stage-program-from-june-27-2024) program and check if you're eligible.

### Why did I get two invoices ?

When migrating to a new pricing plan, two invoices may appear temporarily. The first invoice covers any outstanding charges from the previous plan, while the second includes the seats and add-ons for the upcoming month on the new plan. This does not represent double billing, as the invoices cover different timeframes, and future invoices will consolidate to a single charge.

### Can I pay in my local currency?

Intercom is billed in United States Dollars (USD) only. This applies across our whole platform, including seat pricing, additional usage charges, add-ons, and Premier Services. Right now, we can’t bill in any other currencies.

### What should I do if my account has been restricted due to non-payment of invoices?

We will attempt to process payment on invoices up to four times within a 28-day period. If all four attempts fail, the account will be frozen on the 28th day. To resume access, all unpaid invoices must be paid.

​

Payment can be made by following the link for payment on the invoice or under [Settings > Subscription > Billing > Invoices](https://app.intercom.com/a/apps/_/settings/subscription/billing/invoices) using the **Pay Now** button. ​

​

Once payment for all open invoices is completed, your account access will be restored. You may need to hard refresh your browser to see services resume on your workspace.

### Will I be charged for sending messages through Workflows?

No, we do not charge for messages sent in a [workflow](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/7836459-workflows-explained). Charges apply to bulk outbound messaging set up from the Outbound section of your workspace.

### What is your Billing / Finance team e-mail?

You can contact Intercom’s Billing team at **accountsreceivable@intercom.io** for any questions related to invoices, payments, or billing verification.

Alternatively, you can start a conversation with our team directly via chat.

## Free trials

### How does the free trial work?

Intercom’s free trial requires no credit card to sign up. During the 14-day trial, you’ll have access to all features in your chosen plan, as well as free Fin AI Agent and the Pro, Copilot, and Proactive Support Plus add-ons. After the free trial period, just add your credit card details to continue using Intercom.

**Note:** If your trial was set up by our Sales team, you'll need to contact your Account Manager to start a paid subscription—Sales-led trials don't automatically convert to paid plans when you add payment details.

### Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

No! You do not need to enter any payment details to start a free trial with Intercom and there are no setup fees. For self-serve trials, a card is required to continue using Intercom after your free trial. If your trial was set up by our Sales team, you'll need to contact your Account Manager instead of adding a payment method.

### What happens at the end of my free trial?

At the end of your free trial, you'll no longer be able to use Intercom until you start a paid subscription:

*   If you signed up for a self-serve trial on your own, you can choose your plan and add a payment method to continue.

*   If your trial was set up by our Sales team, you'll need to contact your Account Manager to start your paid subscription—Sales-led trials require Sales approval and don't automatically convert when you add payment details.

### Can I start a free trial of Fin for Intercom?

Yes, all customers can start a free trial of Fin AI Agent at any time (even after your initial 14-day trial of Intercom has ended). To start a free trial, head to [Fin AI Agent](https://app.intercom.com/a/apps/_/fin-ai-agent/setup?activeTab=add-content) in your workspace or talk to our team. You won't be charged for Fin usage or resolutions during the trial period.

### Can I start a free trial of Fin for my existing helpdesk?

Yes, if you'd like to use Fin AI Agent on your current platform (Zendesk, Salesforce, and more) you can! Trials require no credit card to sign up. During the 14-day trial, you’ll have unlimited access to free Fin resolutions, with no limits on usage.

### Can I trial Fin again after my free trial has ended?

You can request to trial Fin again—or extend your free trial period—by contacting us in the Messenger.

### How can I extend my free trial or request a new trial?

Eligible customers on a cardless trial may be offered a free 7-day self-serve trial extension from Day 13 onwards. This option, if available, will appear in your trial banner, on the expired state screen, or in emails from us. You can activate the extension whenever you're ready — the 7-day clock starts when you choose to activate it. If you don't see the self-serve extension option or have already used it, you can request to extend your trial by contacting our support team directly in the Messenger. To ensure your request is routed to a human, please type the specific phrase:

*   "I need to extend my trial" or

*   "Please connect me to the team to request a trial extension" .

A teammate will then be able to review your account and help you with your request.

### Can I apply my existing referral program benefit (e.g., the 2-month free trial) to a second or additional workspace?

No. Referral program benefits are designed exclusively for **new Intercom customers** and are limited to one per company. You cannot self-apply a referral benefit to your second or additional workspace.

## Subscriptions

### Does Intercom offer a free plan?

### Who is my 'Account Manager'?

Customers who sign a contract for their subscription are assigned an Account Manager at Intercom who helps handle their subscription. If you're not sure who your Intercom Account Manager is, reach out to us in the Messenger and request to 'Talk to a person' so we can put you in touch with them.

### Why can't I choose the number of seats I want when starting a subscription for Essential?

If you have existing teammates on your workspace, you will see a seat count equal to the number of teammates. For example if you have 3 teammates and try start a trial/subscription for Essential, it will show 3 seats ($39 x 3 = $117). 

​

To reduce the seat count, you will need to first [delete the additional teammates](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/280-add-remove-delete-or-export-a-teammate#how-to-remove-teammates).

### Why do I see a payment and refund when I start my new subscription?

When you start your subscription, we’ll place a temporary hold on your credit card to ensure funds are available for your first invoice. The amount will be released immediately, and no money will actually leave your account—it's just a hold that will be removed.

### What happens if I cancel my subscription? Will I lose settings, API keys, custom branding settings, etc.?

Canceling your subscription will not delete any data. However, live content such as Proactive Support messages, Workflows, and your Help Center, will be automatically paused as soon as your subscription becomes inactive. Also, any teammate permissions will be reset.

Title: Proactive Support Plus add-on

URL Source: https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on

Markdown Content:
A number of outbound message channels come included with our core plans (Essential, Advanced, or Expert). This includes [Chats](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/3292781-get-started-sending-chat-and-post-messages), [Banners](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/4557393-how-to-create-a-banner-message) and [Tooltips](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/6475940-tooltips-explained) which are free of charge with all core plans with no usage based cost. Other channels include Email, Whatsapp, SMS and Phone which have their own usage based charges which you can review [here](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels).

**Proactive Support Plus** is an optional add-on to any core plan that you’re on (Essential, Advanced, or Expert) that gives you additional outbound capabilities. It is designed to streamline engagement and improve customer satisfaction with a suite of advanced tools included in the add-on. The add-on integrates seamlessly with Essential, Advanced, and Expert plans, ensuring compatibility and enhanced functionality for any user tier.

If you decide to [purchase this add-on](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8344189-how-to-upgrade-or-downgrade-from-a-plan), you’ll be charged a base fee, plus usage-based charges for some additional channels.

Unless you are on a contract, you can add this to your plan any time. It will be prorated and billed ahead for the current billing period (i.e one month or one year depending on your subscription). If you’re on a contract, chat to your Account Manager. For monthly subscriptions, the add-on can be added or removed anytime with prorated charges based on the change date.

You'll then be able to use Proactive Support Plus features from [Outbound](https://app.intercom.com/a/apps/_/outbound/all).

## Messages Sent

The metric 'Messages Sent' is used to calculate the cost of sending Posts, Mobile Push, Product Tours, Mobile Carousels, and Surveys. Usage of these channels is only available if you have the Proactive Support Plus add-on.

The add-on includes 500 'Messages Sent' each month. If you go over this, you’ll start being charged per message ([see rates below](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on#h_b7bdd5b108)). This allocation ensures that a significant portion of initial engagement can occur without additional charges, making it easier to plan your outreach.

### How to calculate the cost of sending Posts, Mobile Push, Product Tours, Mobile Carousels, and Surveys

These channels use the 'Messages Sent' metric which follows a tiered pricing structure. When your usage exceeds a tier threshold, the cost per message decreases. Charges are determined by the proportion of your usage within each tier.

So to work out the total cost, you’ll need to include all messages sent through any of these channels, then calculate the cost for each tier up to your total volume and then sum these costs.

### Messages Sent pricing examples

_1. This month your Product Tour was presented to 400 users, and you don’t use any other features included in Messages Sent. Your total Messages Sent usage is 400._

You won't incur any usage-based charges for Messages Sent, since you are currently below the included quantity in your Proactive Support Plus add-on.

_2. This month your “Welcome” Post was presented to 100 users. You also sent 400 Surveys. Your total Messages Sent count is 500._

Again, you won't incur any usage-based charges for Messages Sent, since you are within the 500 Messages Sent included in the Proactive Support Plus add-on.

_3. This month your Mobile Carousel was presented to 3,000 users._

So, it would cost $145 for a Mobile Carousel to be presented to 3,000 users.

## Trial options

A 14-day trial of the Proactive Support Plus add-on is available for new users, allowing you to test the capabilities before committing. During the trial, you gain access to all the key features, including advanced tools for proactive support. Trial details, such as activation and end dates, are viewable in Billing Settings under Subscription. Contact Intercom support to initiate the trial.

## Your questions, answered

How does Intercom pricing work?

Pricing depends on whether you’re purchasing our customer service platform with Fin AI Agent, or Fin with your current helpdesk including (Zendesk, Salesforce, and more).

## Pricing for Fin AI Agent on Intercom:

Fin AI Agent is priced at $0.99 per outcome.

An outcome is counted when:

*   Fin resolves a conversation - the customer confirms Fin resolved their issue, or they do not request for more help after Fin answers, or

*   Fin executes a Procedure* - that ends in a resolution or an intentional handoff.

You’ll only be charged once per conversation, even if Fin resolves multiple questions.

*_Applies only to customers using Procedures. Procedures train Fin to handle complex, multi-step customer queries like subscription issues or refunds._

You'll need at least one seat from one of our three plans (Essential, Advanced or Expert) to start using Fin on Intercom.

## Pricing for Intercom's customer service platform:

When purchasing Intercom’s customer service platform, you’ll choose from three plans: Essential, Advanced, or Expert. The plan you choose determines your seat price. You’ll then be charged based on the number of teammates who require Full seats. Full seats provide complete access to all features in your plan and are charged per teammate.

You can learn more about Seat types [here](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205716-seats).

**All our plans include access to:**

Fin AI Agent: $0.99 per outcome—meaning you only pay for the value it delivers.

Messaging channels: Usage-based pricing for SMS, phone, product tours, and more. Learn more [here](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels).

What is Copilot?

Copilot is an AI-powered assistant in the Intercom Inbox that helps customer support agents quickly find answers, troubleshoot issues, and respond to follow-up questions—saving time and allowing them to build stronger customer relationships.

Every Intercom plan includes limited usage (up to 10 Copilot conversations and 10 AI Auto-translation conversations per teammate per month), which resets monthly. For unlimited usage, you can add Copilot to any Full Seat for $35 per seat per month (subject to the Additional Product Terms for AI Products).

Learn more about Copilot [here](https://www.intercom.com/suite/helpdesk/copilot).

What is Proactive Support Plus?

Proactive Support Plus is an optional add-on available for any Intercom plan (Essential, Advanced, or Expert).

It unlocks advanced capabilities like Product Tours, Surveys, In-app Posts, Mobile Push, and Mobile Carousels. The add-on is priced at $99 per month, which includes 500 messages sent each month. If you exceed 500 messages in a month, additional charges will apply based on the ["Messages Sent"](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on#h_45c5377e15) metric.

You can add Proactive Support Plus to your plan at any time unless you are on a contract, in which case you’ll need to contact your Account Manager.

Learn more about Proactive Support Plus [here](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on).

Are there any additional usage charges?

In addition to Seats and ‘Proactive Support Plus’, you only pay for what you use. Here’s a breakdown:

## WhatsApp:

WhatsApp is charged per ‘conversation’, with unit costs varying by volume. We have different pricing for inbound and outbound WhatsApp. [Learn more about WhatsApp pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels#h_13e2dcaf16)

## SMS:

SMS is charged per the number of “SMS sent and/or received” with unit costs varying by volume. Prices also vary by region. [Learn more about SMS pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels#h_ee3403f06b)

## Emails and Email Campaigns:

Emails sent from the Inbox have no additional charge. Inbound emails from your customers and responses to those emails are included in your plan, with no additional charge. Email campaigns are charged per the number of ‘Bulk Emails Sent’, which are emails sent to more than one customer. [Learn more about Email pricing](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061703-usage-based-channels#h_6fdc658267)

How can I change my plan or number of seats?

You can easily upgrade or downgrade between the Essential, Advanced or Expert plans, and add or remove the Proactive Support Plus add-on at any time.

Customers on annual plans can upgrade and manage seats at any time. Downgrades will be processed at renewal.

To add seats, simply head to ‘Settings’ > ‘Teammates’ to add or remove seats. If you have the Advanced or Expert plan, you can also swap a teammate's full Seat for a free Lite Seat. To change your plan, simply head to ‘Billing’ > ‘Subscriptions’.

[Learn more about changing or canceling your plans](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8344189-how-to-manage-your-intercom-subscription)

What’s the difference between a Full and a Lite seat? 

A Full seat provides full access to all Intercom capabilities contained within your chosen plan. Generally speaking, teammates who need to communicate with customers or use core features will need a Full seat.

A Lite seat provides limited access to Intercom to support cross-functional collaboration. A set number of Lite seats are included without additional charge on the Advanced and Expert plans. Note that Lite seats are not included on the Essential plan.

You can learn more about seat types [here](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205716-seats).

How do I change my Intercom plan or number of seats?

Our plans are designed to give you flexibility and control, allowing you to scale your customer service as your needs evolve.

You can easily upgrade or downgrade between the Essential, Advanced, or Expert plans, and add or remove seats as your team changes. Customers on annual plans can upgrade and manage seats at any time, while downgrades will take effect at renewal.

**Managing seats**

*   To add or remove seats, go to Settings > Teammates in your Intercom workspace
*   On the Advanced or Expert plans, you can also swap a teammate's Full seat for a free Lite seat. You can learn more about seat types [here](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8205716-seats).

**Changing plans**

*   To change your plan, go to Billing > Subscriptions in your Intercom workspace.
*   You can adjust your plan or add/remove features like the Proactive Support Plus add-on at any time.

For more details, review our [guide](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/8344189-how-to-manage-your-intercom-subscription) on managing your Intercom subscription.

How is ‘Messages Sent’ calculated?

‘Messages Sent’ is the metric that Intercom uses to calculate charges for certain message types within the Proactive Support Plus add-on. These message types are: Product Tours, Surveys, In-app Posts, Mobile Push, and Mobile Carousels.

‘Messages Sent’ is calculated by totaling all messages sent through Product Tours, Surveys, In-app Posts, Mobile Push, and Mobile Carousels. during a billing month.

The baseline monthly fee for ‘Proactive Support Plus’ includes 500 Messages Sent every month. Usage above this amount will be charged per unit (see additional usage question above).

For example: If you send 1000 Product Tours, 1000 Surveys, and 2000 Mobile Push messages during a billing month, your ‘Messages Sent’ total for that billing month would be 4000. Since the first 500 ‘Messages Sent’ are included each billing month, they are free. Only the additional 3500 Messages Sent’ would be billed as an additional charge.

[Learn more about ‘Messages Sent’](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9061648-proactive-support-plus-add-on)

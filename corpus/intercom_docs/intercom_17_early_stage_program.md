# What is Intercom's Early Stage program?

Intercom's Early Stage program is aimed at helping eligible startups gain access to Intercom at a discounted rate. Launched in 2016, the program has over 10,000 startup customers as alumni. Our goal is to help startups grow with us by offering incremental pricing discounts. This allows startups the time they need to scale up while enjoying the full range of Intercom features.

### Key benefits

*   Access to the **Advanced plan**, **Proactive Support Plus and Pro**. 
*   Enjoy deep discounts for your first three years while scaling your business. 
*   Get up to 315 free Fin outcomes (including 15 qualifications) per month to deliver exceptional customer experiences while dramatically reducing costs. 
*   Learn faster with the Early Stage Academy and the Startups Dealbook, offering over $100k in exclusive partner discounts.

In the first year, you'll be getting this plan for just $65 per month, reflecting a 93% discount off the original list price of $903.

Some usage-based charges still apply for Fin, SMS, outbound WhatsApp, and Phone which are charged at list price. See details below.

## What features are not included?

Any features from our [Expert plan](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) and Regional Hosting are not available for the Early Stage program.

**Important:** Fin beyond included outcomes, SMS, WhatsApp, and Phone usage are not discounted.

If you find the costs are too high in year two and beyond, you can always move to our [Essential plan](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) starting at $39/month for 1 seat or $29/month if you choose the annual option.

# Application process

## Eligibility criteria

*   Early stage: The startup should have raised up to $10 million in funding 
*   Small company: The startup should have fewer than 15 employees 
*   New customer: The startup must currently not be an Intercom customer 

To grow your startup with Intercom’s Early Stage program, [apply here](https://www.intercom.com/early-stage).

Some applications can be automatically approved and others go to review, the approval process typically takes 1 to 2 business days. If you're approved, you'll be notified via email. It's important to provide accurate information for our team to approve your application.

Once approved, you have 7 days to start your subscription. If you don't activate your subscription within 30 days, the application expires and reapplication will be required.

**Note:** US-based nonprofit organizations may be eligible for a nonprofit discount.

# Transitioning from the Early Stage program

## Before switching plans

Switching from Early Stage to another plan (such as Essential) can affect pricing and seat counts. Keep in mind:

*   **Per-seat costs increase** — Essential is billed at $39 per seat. 
*   **Usage discounts end** — messages, emails, and other usage-based items return to list pricing. 

## Migration steps

1.   **Contact Support** to remove your Early Stage plan. Your billing page won’t show new plan options until removal is confirmed. 
2.   **Explore new plans** from your workspace’s **Subscription tab**. 
3.   **Review pricing and billing** — changes take effect in your next billing cycle. 

## Cost optimization tips

*   **Remove inactive teammates** to reduce seat charges. 
*   **Downgrade plans carefully** — Early Stage acts as an annual contract billed monthly, so downgrades apply after your current billing period. 
*   **Use the [Pricing Calculator](https://www.intercom.com/pricing/calculator)** to estimate your costs before switching.

# FAQs

### What happens if I'm not accepted to the program?

If you don't meet the [eligibility criteria](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9361928-early-stage-program#h_dab404af7f) for the Early Stage program, we have [plans available here](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) for all businesses, or you can chat to our Sales team.

### What happens in early stage year two?

You can move to the second year of the Early Stage program with the 50% year two discount applied, or downgrade to an [Essential plan](https://www.intercom.com/phone/pricing) at list price, or chat to our Sales team about the Expert plan. The Essential plan offers pricing at $39/month per seat or $29/month (billed annually). Features include Shared Inbox, Chatbots, email, SMS, WhatsApp, public help centers, and ticketing systems. For more details, use Intercom's [Pricing Calculator](https://www.intercom.com/pricing/calculator).

### What happens in year three?

You can move to the third year of the Early Stage program with the 25% year three discount applied, or downgrade to an [Essential plan](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) at list price, or chat to our Sales team about the Expert plan.

### What happens at the end of the program?

At the end of year three you'll graduate from the Early Stage program onto one of our [plans](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) at list price. 30 days out from your graduation date we will share options. You can choose a different plan if that suits your needs better.

### How do I restart my trial?

If you need to restart your trial or request a refund, please contact Customer Support via the Messenger and ask to talk to our team.

### I’ve started a trial with Intercom, can I still join the program?

Yes, if you've already started an Intercom trial and you meet the Early Stage program [eligibility criteria](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9361928-early-stage-program#h_dab404af7f) you can still [apply here](https://www.intercom.com/early-stage).

### Can existing Intercom customers apply for the early stage?

No, companies with an active Intercom subscription or on an annual plan are not eligible for the Early Stage program. Applicants must be new customers at the time of application.

### Is there a specific requirement to receive program benefits?

Yes, startups must meet all specified criteria outlined in the program terms to qualify for all benefits.

### Can I join the program a second time?

The Early Stage program is a one-time opportunity for each startup. Once you switch from the Early Stage program to a standard plan, you will permanently forfeit any remaining Early Stage discounts or benefits. You cannot rejoin the Early Stage program after leaving it. Once you leave the Early Stage program, you are not eligible to rejoin, even if you choose to upgrade to a more expensive plan like Expert or Advanced.

### Is this a monthly subscription or annual contract?

This is a monthly subscription, you can cancel at any time from your workspace [Billing](https://app.intercom.com/a/apps/_/billing/summary). If you would prefer to explore annual contracts, please reach out to our Sales team in the Messenger.

### Can we change this subscription at any time?

Yes, this is a monthly subscription, you can cancel or upgrade at any time from your workspace [Billing](https://app.intercom.com/a/apps/_/billing/summary). If you would prefer to explore annual contracts, please reach out to our Sales team in the Messenger.

### What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

See how to [delete your workspace data](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/3687072-how-to-delete-your-workspace-data).

### How do I switch from the Early Stage plan to the Essential plan in Intercom?

To downgrade from the Advanced plan on Early Stage to the Essential plan in Intercom, you'll need to [cancel your current subscription](https://www.intercom.com/help/en/articles/9088378-how-to-cancel-your-subscription) and begin a new subscription on the Essential plan.

If you’d prefer to avoid any disruption, we can manually adjust your plan for you. Please note that no refunds will be issued. Instead, a prorated credit will be automatically applied to your next invoice. This credit will reflect the difference between what you paid for your plan from the day you requested the downgrade and the cost of the Essential plan for that same period.

### Are there any limitations to the Early Stage program?

*   **Fixed seat allocation:** The Early Stage program includes six full seats as part of a fixed package. You cannot reduce this number to lower costs or adjust the plan for fewer users. All six seats are billed regardless of actual usage. You can add seats beyond six, and your discount applies for each additional seat. 
*   **Restrictions on add-ons:** Add-ons included in the Early Stage program cannot be removed. Customers needing tailored configurations may need to switch to a plan like Advanced, where add-ons can be managed more flexibly. 
*   **Non-reinstatement:** Once you leave the Early Stage program, reapplying is not an option. This is a one-time plan designed for startups and early-stage businesses. 

### What support does Intercom offer during plan transitions?

Our team can guide you through plan migration, workspace optimization, and best practices for using Intercom tools effectively.

### Does the Early Stage discount apply to the Expert plan?

No, the Early Stage program is only available on the Advanced plan. Expert plan features, including multibrand Messenger, multibrand Help Center, SLAs, HIPAA support, and SSO, are not included in the Early Stage program. If you need Expert features, you'll need to purchase the Expert plan at standard pricing. The Early Stage discount does not apply to Expert.

To grow your startup with Intercom’s Early Stage program, [apply here](https://www.intercom.com/early-stage).

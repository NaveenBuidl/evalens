Switch pricing [legacy plans]
Understand how the number of Calls Deflected is calculated, and how that affects the price of your Intercom subscription.


Written by Beth-Ann Sher
Updated over 12 months ago
This article refers to our legacy pricing plans. If you've joined Intercom since November 15, 2023 this information may not apply to you. For information about our current pricing plans, please view these articles instead.

 

If you have purchased the Switch add-on, your monthly price depends on the number of “Calls Deflected” in your Intercom workspace in the last month.

 

What does “Calls Deflected” mean exactly?
“Calls Deflected” is the total number of SMS sent out to end users from Intercom when they decide to switch their call to messaging. 

 

For example, if a user chooses to switch their call to messaging, they will receive an SMS from Intercom with a link to the Messenger. This will count as 1 Call Deflected.

 

Note: 

After an agent replies to an Intercom Messenger conversation via a Switch deflection, the end user will receive an SMS if the conversation is unread after a period of time (~3 minutes). This SMS will not contribute to your “Calls Deflected”.

 

How does this impact my invoice?
The number of Calls Deflected is shown in your billing summary

 

Note: These numbers may take up to 24 hours to update.
